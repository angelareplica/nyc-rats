A decade of NYC rats

Nearly 140,000 rat sightings have been officially reported to 311 since January 2010. New Yorkers reported 80% more rat sightings in 2017 than 2012, just five years earlier.

*Data through November 11, 2019

Summertime, and the livin's easy (if ur a rat)

Rat complaints typically peak in July and August. Despite a $32 million Neighborhood Rat Reduction plan implemented in 2017, reported sightings aren't down by much.
